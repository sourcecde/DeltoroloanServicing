Basic Loan Servicing (Sub-Servicing)

Our Basic Servicing – Sub-Servicing structure is an excellent value for Brokers or Lenders who are hands-on with their clients and require only scheduled, predominantly automated assistance. Should your files need extra attention, our Specialized Loan Servicing, detailed below, may provide the best results.


$45.00 setup fee - $20.00 per lender, per month


  • Lender / Borrower Welcome Packet
  • Lender / Borrower Monthly Statement
  • Electronic Collection (ACH) from Borrower & electronic deposit of Funds (ACH) to Lender's account
  • End-of-year account summary and 1098 & 1099 preparation and delivery
  • End-of-year account summary and 1098 & 1099 preparation and delivery
  • Online secure account access with up to date information
  • Late notices and collection calls performed on the 15th, 30th, 45th, 60th and 75th day of delinquency


Note: Del Toro retains 50% of late and default charges and 10% of pre-pay penalties. Multi-Lender Loans must select a single point of contact.

  • Delinquent Loans (90 day+)
  • Active Modification / Forbearance
  • Active Modification / Forbearance
